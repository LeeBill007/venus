Organic certified essential oil


Introduction and official certification

Plants grown in an organic environment, water, soil, and air are subject to strict safety control and pollution monitoring, and the plant's growth environment is purified, and all indicators meet organic standards. Plants grown in such an environment are healthy and non-polluting, and the essential oils extracted from them are organic essential oils. The United States Department of Agriculture(USDA)頒發“有機證書"。

Method of acquisition

  • Organic essential oils are volatile aromatic substances extracted from the flowers, leaves, stems, roots or fruits of plants by steam distillation, extrusion, cold soaking or extraction.
  • The extraction requirements of organic plant essential oils are extremely strict, and the extraction mode of solvent extraction and alcohol extraction is refused to ensure the quality of essential oils.

Therefore, the process of compound essential oil relies heavily on the recognition, extraction, distillation and interaction of different plants to achieve a balanced balance.
