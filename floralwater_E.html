The formation of flower water is in the process of distilling flowers and plants to obtain essential oil, and the distilled water obtained by condensation condensation is condensed into a small crystal water droplet in the condensation tube. Therefore, flower water can be said to be a by-product of essential oils. In addition to containing trace amounts of essential oils, flower water contains many water-soluble substances in plants, which are lacking in general essential oils. Huashui is the most natural moisturizing skin care product. It is mild and non-irritating to the skin. Therefore, it can be used directly without dilution. It can be used in spray bottles, sprayers or facial masks.
